How to convert 37.7 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit?

To convert 37.7 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit, you can use the formula:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

So for 37.7 degrees Celsius:

°F = (37.7 × 9/5) + 32 = 99.86 degrees Fahrenheit.

Fever Treatment Advice

If your child has a fever of 37.7°C (99.86°F), follow these steps:

  • Hydration: Offer plenty of fluids to prevent dehydration.
  • Comfort: Dress your child in light clothing and keep the room cool.
  • Medication: Use fever-reducing medication (e.g., acetaminophen) only if recommended by a doctor.
  • When to See a Doctor: If the fever lasts more than 24 hours or is accompanied by other symptoms (e.g., vomiting, rash).
