How to convert 26 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit?

To convert 26 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit, you can use the formula:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

So for 26 degrees Celsius:

°F = (26 × 9/5) + 32 = 78.8 degrees Fahrenheit.
