How to convert 230 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit?

To convert 230 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit, you can use the formula:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

So for 230 degrees Celsius:

°F = (230 × 9/5) + 32 = 446 degrees Fahrenheit.
