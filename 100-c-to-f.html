Conversion Formula Breakdown

The scientific formula:

°F = (100°C × 9/5) + 32 = 212°F

Step-by-step calculation:

1. Multiply Celsius value by 9/5: 100 × 1.8 = 180

2. Add 32 to the result: 180 + 32 = 212

3. Final result: 100°C = 212°F
