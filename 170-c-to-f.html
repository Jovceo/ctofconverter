How many degrees Fahrenheit is 170 degrees Celsius?

170°C equals 338°F

Temperature Conversion Formula

Convert from Celsius to Fahrenheit:

°F = °C × 1.8 + 32

Example:

170°C = 170 × 1.8 + 32 = 338°F

190°C = 374°F

Applications of 170°C in Kitchen Equipment

Oven

170°C is commonly used for baking and roasting:

  • Baking bread: Achieves a golden and crispy surface.
  • Roasting meat: Keeps the interior juicy while roasting the surface just right.

Note: Preheat the oven and use an oven thermometer to calibrate the temperature.

Air Fryer

170°C is ideal for:

  • French fries: Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside.
  • Chicken wings: Bake for 20-25 minutes at 170°C.

Tip: Air fryers are hotter than conventional ovens; adjust the temperature and cooking time accordingly.

Convection Oven

170°C in a convection oven is suitable for:

  • Baking cakes: Even heating ensures a perfect bake.
  • Roasted vegetables: Retains nutrients while roasting.

Precautions: Convection ovens may require a 5-10°C lower temperature setting.

Instructions for Use

Fan Oven: Fan ovens circulate heat through the air, so temperatures are typically about 20°C lower than conventional ovens. For example, a conventional oven set to 190°C should be set to 170°C for a fan oven.

Gas Mark: Gas ovens are marked in 25°F increments and are typically used in the UK, Ireland, and some Commonwealth countries.

Suggestions for Oven Temperature Adjustment

Calibration: If your oven temperature is inaccurate, it is recommended that you use an oven thermometer for calibration.

Cooking Time: Fan ovens cook faster due to the even distribution of heat, so you may need to lower the temperature or shorten the cooking time appropriately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What kind of food is suitable for baking at 170°C?

A: 170°C is suitable for baking bread, chicken wings, french fries, vegetables, etc. Adjust cooking time based on the type and thickness of the food.

Q2: How to calibrate the oven temperature?

A: Use an oven thermometer to check the actual temperature after preheating. Adjust the oven temperature setting if there is a deviation.

Q3: Does 170°C need to be adjusted in the air fryer?

A: Air fryers are usually hotter than conventional ovens. Set the temperature to 160-165°C and reduce cooking time appropriately.
