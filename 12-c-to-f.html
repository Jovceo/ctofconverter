How to convert 12 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit?

To convert 12 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit, you can use the formula:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

So for 12 degrees Celsius:

°F = (12 × 9/5) + 32 = 53.6 degrees Fahrenheit.
