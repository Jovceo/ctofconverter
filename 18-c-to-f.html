How to convert 18 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit?

To convert 18 degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit, you can use the formula:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

So for 18 degrees Celsius:

°F = (18 × 9/5) + 32 = 64.4 degrees Fahrenheit.
