How to Convert 120 Celsius to Fahrenheit: Exact Formula

To convert from Celsius to Fahrenheit, use the following formula:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Step-by-Step Calculation

  1. Start with the temperature in Celsius: 120°C
  2. Multiply by 9/5: 120 × 9/5 = 120 × 1.8 = 216
  3. Add 32: 216 + 32 = 248
  4. Result: 120°C = 248°F
