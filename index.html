Calculation Method

Celsius to Fahrenheit Conversion Formula:

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Here are the steps to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit:

Step 1: Multiply the Celsius temperature by 9/5.

Step 2: Add 32 to the result.

Step 3: The final result is the temperature in Fahrenheit.

Why Convert Celsius to Fahrenheit?

Celsius (°C): The metric unit of temperature used in most countries.

Fahrenheit (°F): The imperial unit of temperature primarily used in the United States.

Understanding how to convert between these two temperature scales is useful for:

• Traveling: When visiting countries that use different temperature scales.

• Cooking: Many recipes use Fahrenheit, especially in the U.S.

• Weather: Understanding temperature forecasts in different regions.

• Science: Converting temperatures for scientific calculations.
